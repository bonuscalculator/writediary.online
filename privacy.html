Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At WriteDiary.online, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our secure digital diary service.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide you with a functional journaling experience:

  • Diary Entries: The content you write, including titles, text, tags, and mood selections.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication purposes.
  • Usage Data: Basic technical information like browser type and session duration to help us improve the app.

2. How Your Data is Used

Your diary entries are strictly private. We do not read, sell, or share your personal writing with third parties. Data is used solely to:

  • Display and organize your entries within the app.
  • Provide mood tracking and personal insights.
  • Enable search and filtering of your own content.

3. Security & Encryption

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your diary entries are stored securely, ensuring that only you can access your personal journal.

4. Data Ownership

You own your data. You can delete individual entries or your entire account at any time. When you delete data, it is permanently removed from our active servers.

5. Cookies

We use small cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences, such as your chosen editor font or theme settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@writediary.online.
